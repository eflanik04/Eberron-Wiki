Khorvaire

A location on the world of Eberron

The Nations

There are 5 official nations of Khorvaire, although there are many more areas to this continent. The nations include Aundair, Breland, Karrnath, Thrane, and the Mournland.

Other Bordered Lands

Other bordered lands in Khorvaire include The Demon Wastes, the Shadow Marches, Droaam, Eldeen Reaches, Zilargo, Darguun, the Talenta Plains, Q'Barra, Valenar, Mror Holds, and the coast of the Lhazaar Principalities.

Geography

Khorvaire is located in the northern hemisphere on Eberron. It neighbors the Bitter Seato the north, the Lhazaar Sea to the east, the Barren Seato the west, and the Thunder Seato the south. The landscape varies greatly, from wastelands and mountains to the north, to jungles and deserts in the south.

Races and Cultures

Humans

While not native to Khorvaire, humans dominate the continent. Humans founded the Five Nations. The largest populations can be found in Aundair, Breland, Karrnath, The Lhazaar Principalities, and Thrane.

Dwarves

Along with humans, dwarves are also migrants to Khorvaire. Dwarves orignally migrated to the Mror Holds, far before the arrival of humanity. Large dwarven settlements can be found there, however there are some that can be found in other various parts of Khorvaire. The crafty dwarves founded the House Kundarak to manage banking and financial institutes.

Elves

Elves are also non native to Khorvaire. They can currently be found in several main locations across Khorvaire:

  • Aerenal: These elves hailed from the island subcontinent with the same name. There are no large settlements of Aerenal elves, however they can be found in small numbers near nearly every city.
  • Valenar: These elves , more specifically Tairnadal, hail in the nation of Valenar.
  • Phiarlan and Thuranni: These elves make up two dragonmark houses, and can be found wherever their enclaves are.
Gnomes

Gnomes are native to Zilargo, but can be found throughout the continent. The Gnomish House Sivis works in diplomacy and communications, helping keep the greater society of Khorvaire intact.

Goblinoids

The goblinoids are composed of 3 main races: Goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears. These races once dominated the continent of Khorvaire, due to their origins there. While many no longer have control over the continent, some are attempting to rebuild in Darguun. Many goblinoids can also be found in the nation of Droaam. Goblins exist as an underclass in most cities, while hobgoblins and bugbears are employed as mercenaries for the House of Deneith.

Half-Elves

Half Elves are common in Khorvaire as a distinct race, known as the Khoravar due to their Khorvairian origins. Many are associated with the Dragonmark House of Medani and Lyrandar.

Halflings

Halflings are native to the Talenta Plains. These halflings tend to be very traditional, and tribal.

Orcish

Orcs and their progeny, half-orcs, inhabit the western parts of Khorvaire, and have so for a millennia. They can primarily be found in the Shadow Marches, Demon Wastes, and Droaam.
