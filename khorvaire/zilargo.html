Geography and Features

Topography

Most of Zilargo has been settled on by the gnomes. Before settlement, Zilargo was a beautiful mix of coast, plains, and rugged mountains.

Borders

  • Thunder Sea to the South
  • Howling Peaks to the Northwest
  • Breland to the North and West

Transportation

Lightning rails pass along the eastern border, stopping in Zolanberg and Korranberg. There are also House Orien railways present throughout the Nation.

Exclusive to Zilargo

The Deep Mine

The Deep Mine is a platinum mine set in the northeastern part of Zilargo, in the Seawall Mountains. The Deep Mine is host to the most sophisticated mining team, a collaboration between the Dragonmark Houses and the Dwarvish clan Mroranon. Moranon provides most of the labor, mining the pure platinum and smelting it into perfect ingots. House Kundarak will purchase the ingots, using them as internal currency. These mines have been known to disturb the monsters of Khyber, and attract the attention of thieves and bandits. Therefore, House Kundarak provides great levels of security. House Sivis handles relations between the working forces of the Mine and the goverment of Zilargo.

History

Zilargo was inhabited by the gnomes for thousands of years, well into the Dhakaani Empire and Daelkyr War. At one point, Zilargo was taken over by the Dhakaani Empire, however the gnomes quickly reclaimed their territory during the Daelkyr War. From the ashes of the collapse of the Dhakaani Empire rose 3 gnomish city-states: Korranberg, Trolanport and Zolanberg.

Eventually these city-states became under attack from Maellon the Reaver and Karrn the Conquerer, forcing them to innovate to survive. The gnomes built their first form of government, the democracy of The Triumvirate, and the nation of Zilargo. The Trust was also created as a secret body of police to protect the nation.

During the reign of Galifar ir'Wynarn I., Zilargo surrendered to his subjugation and negotiated the status of an independant province to the Kingdom of Galifar. During the first part of The Last War, Zilargo remained loyal to the Kingdom of Galifar. However their political stance changed in 962 YK for an unknown reason, declaring itself loyal to Breland.

In 996 YK, the Treaty of Thronehold declared Zilargo a recognized nation of Khorvaire.

Civilization

The people of Zilargo are thirsty for knowledge, from the secrets of the universe to the juiciest gossip. Their desire for knowledge creates a environment filled with deceit, blackmail, deals, and manipulation.

Dragonroost

A small town in northern Zilargo. It is the first settlement you will hit upon entry from Breland on the House Orien trade routes. It was in a small valley in the city of Dragonroost that the gnomes made dealings with Galifar ir'Wynarn I.

Korranberg

As the oldest city of Zilargo, Korranberg is home to the vaunted Library of Korranberg, which is arguably the greatest collection of knowledge on the world of Eberron, and is amongst the best universities. Both House Orien Trade Routes and the lightning rain pass through here. Korranberg is also a central point to The Triumvirate, which originally rules from Trolanport. The city itself is administered by a Council of Nine, where the city's triumvir is chosen. House Sivis most ancient enclave resides here.

The Labyrinth

The Labyrinth is the head enclave for House Sivis. This historical enclave was built directly above the many cavernous tunnels used by the Dhakaani Empire. Today, these caverns are host to the many vaults used by House Sivis, holding records of vital communications.

Liugwin

A city host to the House Orien trade route, approximately halfway between Trolanport and Oskilor.

Oskilor

A large port town in the center of Zilargo. Oskilor lies on the House Orien trade route. The Sharn Inquisitive recieves intel from.

Quesk

A cillage in central Zilargo. It lies on the crossing of both House Orien trade routes that pass through the nation.

Trolanport

The capital city of Zilargo, built next to the Trolan River. Trolanport was build on the ruined foundations of the Dhakaani Empire. The port is designed with a canal system, and is the central hub for gnomish shipbuilding of both naval and aerial types. Trolanport a central trade point of Khorvaire, and is a major departure port for Xen'drik. Aerenal, and Sarlona. While all Dragonmark Houses have presence in Trolanport, House Kundarak, House Sivis, and House Cannith have major enclaves here.

Zolanberg

A small city in the Seawall Mountains, known for its mining operations. Zolanberg is one of the three major cities that make up The Triumvirate. This city hosts both the Orien Trade Route and the lightning rail. Zolanberg is governed by a Council of Nine, whos representatives are pulled from the wealthy elite. It is within this council that the triumvir of Zolanberg is chosen. House Kundarak has a major presence, including a large vault. House Tharashk also has a Prospectors Guild vault located here.

Korranberg Chronicle

The most widely read source written in all of Khorvaire, and perhaps all of Eberron. The paper originated in Zilargo, and has worked hard to maintain a reputation of neutrality and objectivity. It is printed in Korranberg, but covers news from all over Khorvaire.

The Chronicle heavily covered the peace process during the end of The Last War, and is held in contempt by King Kaius III of Karrnath. He blames the Chronicle for a number of setbacks he suffered, and thus many reporters face harassment when operating in Karrnath. Similar grievances are held by the Boromar Clan and House Thuranni.

The Korranberg Library

The Library of Korranberg is a research library standing as the largest repository of knowlegde in Khorvaire. The library's buildings take up a quarter of the city of Korranberg, and is a great source of gnomish pride. The library also has eight colleges, each focusing on a particular field of study that attracts students from across the continent. The library is constantly expanding as it gathers more knowledge and improves its resources.

History

The Library was founded by Loremaster Dorius Alyre ir'Korran long before the founding of the Kingdom of Galifar. Dorius was an adherent to the belief that deities of the Sovereign Host were dragons that ascended to godhood, and thus created the library in hope that he himself might ascent to take the place of Aureon. His family and the city-states of Zilargo supported his efforts, intrigued by the idea of having the greatest library in the world. The Trust is tasked with guarding the library.

Leadership

The Library is run by a central council made up of the doyens of the eight colleges of the library. The head of the council is always an appointed member of the Korran family, currently held by Alina Alrene ir'Korran. The eight colleges and their subjects are:

  • Aureon's Holt (law and oratory)
  • Balinor's Horn (natural sciences)
  • Blackdragon (alchemy)
  • Drystone (engineering)
  • Lyrris (art and literature)
  • Morridan (mathematics)
  • Soladas (history)
  • The Tabernacle (religion and philosophy)

The Triumvirate

The governing body of Zilargo. It was originally formed to govern the Library of Korranberg, but was chosen as the general form of government to unite the original three city-states of Zilargo against Maellon the Reaver. The Triumvirate is composed of three Triumvir chosen from Korranberg, Trolanport, and Zolanberg. Most Triumvirs are chosen from the Council of Nine, the local government of each city.

The Trust

A national, non-military organization charged with the safety and security of Zilargo. The Trust acts as a secret police, often dressing in plain clothing and acting like regular folk, while their head is on swivel for any crime or conspiracy. The Trust acknowledges the shared virtues of ingenuity, curiosity, love of family, and overcoming adversity through wit and wisdom. The Trust has jurisdiction over punishing those it determines guilty of transgression, and the justice is swift and merciless. There is no court of law, and any Zil citizen could be an agent of The Trust without anyone knowing. Because of this, Zilargo has the lowest crime rate in all of Khorvaire.

History

The security agency began in Korranberg as an outgrowth of the Korranberg Library. The founder of the organization is only remembered by her title of "The Proctor", and all leaders since have gone by this title.

Although the gnomes allied with each other, this did not stop the feud between and within the different city-states. The Trust was created in order to keep the nation straight and together. Each of the three Triumvirs was given the power to select agents from their city to create this secret agency. The Trust soon secured total control over law enforcement and national security in Zilargo.
