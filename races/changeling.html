Origin - Lifestyles

About 1300 years ago in early Khorvaire, the leaders of the precursor nations of Cyre, Karrnath, and Thrane launched a genocidal attack against changelings within their borders. This forced many to flee to the precursor lands of Aundair and Breland to live in secrecy.

Long after, a changeling known as Kel become unhappy with the constant hiding the changelings were forced into. He missioned across Khorvaire to find other changeling covens, and began a gradual migration to the Lhazaar Principalities before settling on Lastpoint.

Creation

  • There is speculation that changelings resulted from the interbreeding of humans and doppelgangers. As the offspring evolved, they formed into their own distinct race.
  • Another popular theory is that changelings originated first, and doppelgangers were modeled after their talents.
  • Another belief states that the first changeling, tiefling, and skulk bloodlines began amongst the sages of the kingdom of Ohr Kaluun, by making body altering pacts will fell beings.
  • Some changeling priests tell a faithful story about a woman named Jes, with 100 children. In times of trouble, she sought an audience with the Sovereign Host, but was met with silence. Eventually, a lone traveler came and promised to protect Jes' children, only if they followed the travelers path. Accepting Jes, she was given a cloak that she spread over her hundred children. Though their former faces melted away, they had the ability to look like whoever they wanted, becoming the changeling race.
  • Others speculate that this traveler channeled his will and desires into doppelgangers to create a new race for an unknown purpose.
  • Finally, some believe changelings to be a magical manifestation starting in the Feywild.

Appearance

In their natural form, they look human, but appear faded or lacking detail, like an unfinished painting. They appear to have a gray, gauzy extra layer of skin over their bodies. Their skin tone is pale, typically white or light grey, and their hair is thin and fair, commonly silver-white, platinum blond, with some traces of green, pink, or blue. They have blank, colorless eyes that appear sunken. Their other features are barely noticeable, and typically expressionless. They typically stand at 5-6ft, and are usually frail and gangly bodied.

Personality

Most changelings adopt a few personas to serve them in different times of need. They can be of any moral stance, but many face pragmatic neutrality, worrying about their own needs and issues. They are largely uninterested in social interactions. They believe that life is short, and they should make the most of it. A changeling will not "make do", they will maximize their current experience, and try to live a most leisurely life. These creatures excel at learning new languages, and have an appreciation for magic and technology similar to their powers.
