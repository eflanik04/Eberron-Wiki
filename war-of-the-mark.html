War of the Mark

-1500 - -1497
Most of the historical recollection of the War is told from the perspective of the Dragonmarked Houses, and therefore cannot be 100% accurate accounts.

Participants

The War of the Mark was fought between the twelve Dragonmark Houses, and the Aberrant Marks.

Columns
Column

Cause

The Dragonmark Houses feared the devastating power of the
Aberrant Marks, and sought to genocide them.

Column

Result

Almost if not all Aberrant Marks were destroyed by the houses. They are now often feared and live in secret.

The Earthshaker

In the third year of the war, Lord Halas Tarkanan began organizing the aberrants into a military force to oppose the houses. He was accompanied by the Lady of Plague. Though she is commonly seen as a monster, historical records say that she loathed using her abilities unless forced. While Tarkanan's efforts extended the conflicts, the aberrants eventually fell. In a final effort, both Tarkanan and Lady of Plague unleashed the power of their marks that ultimately destroyed the city of Sharn.
